Pattara (Pat) Sukprasert

In Thai alphabets: ภัทร สุขประเสริฐ

I am a third year CS PhD student at Northwestern University. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Samir Khuller.

I am interested in combinatorial optimization, approximation algorithms and graph theory.
I have worked on network design problems, network flow problems, and some structural graph theory problems. Recently, I have also developed some interests in fast (up to sub-cubic) approximation algorithms.
